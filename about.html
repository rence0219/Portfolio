Why Anime?

Anime is popular because it is a powerful and versatile medium that offers unlimited visual creativity and tells deep, diverse, and complex stories for every possible audience, covering every genre from light-hearted comedy to serious psychological drama. Its unique artistic style and commitment to exploring complex themes set it apart from most Western animation.
